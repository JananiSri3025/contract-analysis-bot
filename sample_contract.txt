SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement (“Agreement”) is made on 1 January 2025 between:

ABC Pvt Ltd, a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, having its registered office at Chennai, India (“Client”)

AND

XYZ Services, a partnership firm having its principal place of business at Coimbatore, India (“Service Provider”).

1. Scope of Work
The Service Provider shall provide software development, maintenance, and support services as per the requirements of the Client.

2. Deliverables
The Service Provider shall deliver the project milestones as agreed in writing by both parties.

3. Payment Terms
The Client shall pay a total consideration of ₹5,00,000 within 30 days from the date of invoice.

4. Taxes
All applicable taxes including GST shall be borne by the Client as per prevailing laws.

5. Term of Agreement
This Agreement shall be valid for a period of 12 months from the effective date.

6. Termination for Convenience
The Client may terminate this Agreement at any time without assigning any reason by giving written notice.

7. Termination for Cause
Either party may terminate this Agreement if the other party breaches any material terms and fails to cure such breach within 15 days.

8. Penalty Clause
In case of delay in delivery, the Service Provider shall pay a penalty of ₹10,000 per week of delay.

9. Indemnity
The Service Provider shall indemnify and hold harmless the Client against all losses, claims, and damages.

10. Confidentiality
Both parties shall maintain confidentiality of all proprietary and business information.

11. Intellectual Property Rights
All intellectual property developed under this Agreement shall belong exclusively to the Client.

12. Non-Compete
The Service Provider shall not engage in any competing business for a period of 2 years after termination.

13. Subcontracting
The Service Provider shall not subcontract any part of the work without prior written consent of the Client.

14. Force Majeure
Neither party shall be liable for failure due to events beyond reasonable control.

15. Arbitration
Any dispute arising out of this Agreement shall be resolved through arbitration in Chennai.

16. Governing Law
This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India.

17. Jurisdiction
The courts of Chennai shall have exclusive jurisdiction.

18. Assignment
The Service Provider shall not assign this Agreement without written consent of the Client.

19. Audit Rights
The Client shall have the right to audit records related to this Agreement.

20. Limitation of Liability
The Service Provider’s liability shall not exceed the total contract value.

21. Auto-Renewal
This Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one-year terms unless terminated.

22. Entire Agreement
This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the parties.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement on the date first written above.
