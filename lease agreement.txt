LEASE AGREEMENT

This Lease Agreement is executed on
5 April 2025 between Mr. Ramesh
and BrightStart Pvt Ltd.

1. Property
The leased property is located in Coimbatore.

2. Lease Period
The lease shall be for a period of 5 years
with a lock-in period of 2 years.

3. Rent
Monthly rent shall be ₹60,000
payable on or before 5th of every month.

4. Security Deposit
A refundable deposit of ₹3,00,000
shall be paid by the lessee.

5. Termination
Early termination by lessee
shall attract penalty of 6 months rent.

6. Governing Law
This agreement shall be governed
by the laws of India.
