VENDOR AGREEMENT

This Vendor Agreement is made on
10 March 2025 between RetailMart Pvt Ltd
and FastSupply Vendors.

1. Supply of Goods
The vendor shall supply grocery items
as per purchase orders issued.

2. Quality Standards
All goods must meet quality standards
specified by RetailMart Pvt Ltd.

3. Pricing
Prices shall remain fixed for one year.

4. Payment
Payments shall be released within 45 days
from invoice date.

5. Indemnity
The vendor shall indemnify RetailMart Pvt Ltd
against all losses and damages.

6. Termination
RetailMart Pvt Ltd may terminate
this agreement without notice.

7. Dispute Resolution
Any disputes shall be resolved
through arbitration in Mumbai.

8. Jurisdiction
Courts of Mumbai shall have jurisdiction.
